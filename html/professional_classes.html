PROFESSIONAL

The professional dance school "AKTINA" was founded in September 2013 and operates under the artistic direction of Betty Dramisioti. It consists of a set of distinguished dancers, teachers and dance theorists, proposing an alternative, organized model of education with an emphasis on the development of personal artistic expression.
